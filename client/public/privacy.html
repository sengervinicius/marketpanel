Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 1, 2026

ARC Capital ("we", "us", "our") operates the Senger Market application (the "Service"). This page informs you of our policies regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of personal data when you use our Service.

Information We Collect

Account data: When you create an account, we collect your email address, username, and an encrypted password hash. We do not store plaintext passwords.

Portfolio data: Ticker symbols, quantities, and entry prices you add to your portfolio are stored on our servers to enable cross-device sync.

Alert data: Price alert configurations you create are stored to deliver notifications.

Usage data: We collect anonymous usage metrics (pages viewed, features used) to improve the Service. We do not track your browsing activity outside the app.

How We Use Your Data

We use your data to provide and maintain the Service, send alert notifications, sync your portfolio across devices, and improve our product. We do not sell your personal data to third parties.

Market Data

Market data displayed in the app is sourced from third-party providers. This data is provided for informational purposes only and should not be relied upon for investment decisions. We are not responsible for the accuracy, completeness, or timeliness of market data.

Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored on secure servers hosted by Render, Inc. and MongoDB Atlas. We use industry-standard encryption (TLS/HTTPS) for data in transit and encrypt sensitive data at rest. JWT tokens are used for authentication.

Third-Party Services

We use Stripe for payment processing. When you subscribe, your payment information is handled directly by Stripe and is not stored on our servers. Please review Stripe's Privacy Policy.

Data Retention and Deletion

We retain your account data for as long as your account is active. You may request deletion of your account and all associated data at any time through the app's Settings menu or by contacting us. Upon deletion, your data is permanently removed from our servers within 30 days.

Your Rights

You have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal data. You may export your portfolio data from the app at any time. To exercise these rights, use the in-app settings or contact us at the address below.

Children's Privacy

The Service is not intended for use by anyone under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date.

Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at privacy@senger.market.

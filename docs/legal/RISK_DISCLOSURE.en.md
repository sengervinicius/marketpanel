## Capital at risk

All investments carry risk. Different asset classes carry different risks:

- **Equities** can lose value rapidly in response to earnings, macro, or
  idiosyncratic events; small-cap equities can be illiquid.
- **Fixed income** is subject to interest-rate risk, credit risk, and
  duration risk; emerging-market debt (including Brazilian sovereign
  debt) carries additional sovereign and currency risk.
- **Foreign exchange** is leveraged implicitly through base-currency
  exposure; emerging-market currencies (including BRL) can move sharply.
- **Derivatives** (options, futures) can magnify losses beyond the initial
  capital committed and may result in total loss of margin.
- **Crypto-assets** are unregulated in most jurisdictions, extremely
  volatile, and can become worthless. The FCA considers crypto-assets
  high risk.

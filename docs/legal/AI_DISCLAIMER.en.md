# AI Disclaimer

**Last updated:** 2026-04-18
**Status:** baseline — **DRAFT, NOT YET LEGALLY REVIEWED**.

Particle Terminal uses artificial intelligence to generate summaries,
analyses, and conversational responses. This page explains what the AI
does, what it does not do, and what you should keep in mind.

## What the AI does

- Summarises public market data and news you have access to in Particle.
- Answers research questions using retrieval-augmented generation over
  your knowledge vault and public market data.
- Helps you structure questions and compare instruments.

## What the AI does not do

- **It does not provide investment advice.** Any output that appears to
  recommend a specific action (buy / sell / hold a particular instrument)
  must be treated as general information for your own research, not as a
  personalised recommendation.
- **It does not execute trades.**
- **It does not know your full financial situation, goals, tax position,
  or risk tolerance**, even if you share some of that information in
  conversation.
- **It does not guarantee accuracy.** Large language models can produce
  plausible-sounding but incorrect output ("hallucinations"). Verify any
  claim that would affect a decision.

## How we use AI responsibly

- We log every AI interaction for safety and quality review.
- We apply guardrails to prevent the model from producing harmful or
  deceptive content. Our prompt-injection defences are tested against a
  red-team corpus.
- We do not use your conversation data to train third-party models.
  Our LLM providers (Anthropic, OpenAI) are contractually barred from
  training on our data.
- If you believe an AI response is harmful, misleading, or
  inappropriate, please use the "flag this response" button (coming
  soon) or email support@particle.xyz.

## Your responsibilities

- Verify claims before acting on them.
- Do not share personal or confidential information in chat that you
  would not want stored on a third-party provider.
- Do not upload documents to the vault that you do not have the right to
  share.

# Cookie Notice

**Last updated:** 2026-04-18
**Status:** baseline — **DRAFT, NOT YET LEGALLY REVIEWED**.

This notice explains how Particle Terminal uses cookies and similar
technologies (localStorage, sessionStorage).

## Your controls

You can accept all, reject non-essential, or customise cookies using the
banner shown on your first visit. You can change your preferences at any
time using the "Cookie preferences" link in the footer.

## Cookie categories

### Strictly necessary (always on)

These are required for Particle to function and cannot be switched off.

| Name | Purpose | Expiry |
|---|---|---|
| `auth_token` | Keeps you signed in (JWT, HTTP-only) | 7 days |
| `csrf_token` | CSRF protection | session |
| `session_id` | Server session tracking | session |
| `lgpd_consent_v1` | Records your cookie preferences (localStorage) | 12 months |

### Functional (on by default)

Improve the product experience. You can turn these off.

| Name | Purpose | Expiry |
|---|---|---|
| `layout_prefs` | Remembers your panel layout | 1 year |
| `theme` | Remembers your light/dark preference | 1 year |
| `locale` | Remembers your language preference | 1 year |

### Analytics (opt-in)

Help us understand how the product is used. Off by default; no data is
sent unless you opt in.

| Name | Purpose | Expiry |
|---|---|---|
| `ph_*` (PostHog) | Product analytics | 1 year |

### Marketing (opt-in)

Off by default. Currently we do not run marketing cookies. If this
changes we will list the specific cookies here and re-prompt for consent.

## Third-party cookies

When the Crisp support widget is loaded, it sets its own cookies to
maintain chat state. The widget is only loaded if you open it, so it
does not set cookies unless you initiate support contact.

## Do Not Track and Global Privacy Control

We honour the Global Privacy Control (GPC) signal. If your browser sends
GPC, we treat it as a valid opt-out of analytics and marketing cookies.
